CNC Machine Record Player Stand & Storage (Dec 2024)

for CNC MACHINING with Neva Kocic (Fall 2024): Final Project

In July 2024, I moved to a studio apartment. My friend, Anna, gifted me a record player as a housewarming present. I used to put it on top of a shelf I got from Facebook Marketplace a few years ago, but have always wanted to have a more specific stand for it. So for my CNC Machining class final project, I made my very own turntable stand + record storage for the record player in my apartment.

Made of 1/2" Baltic Birch Plywood.
Depth: 25.5”
Width: 15”
Height 1st Tier: 14”
Height 2nd Tier: 25"
