A little more about Offset 2025 Art Book Fair:
Parsons Communication Design is thrilled to announce the Second Annual Offset Art Book Fair, hosted in partnership with Draw Down Books. You'll find us in 2 W 13th St. on 5th Avenue– on the sixth floor's main atrium space.

Offset facilitates the exhibition & vending of posters, prints, books, and other ephemera; with the effort of highlighting and supporting small press publishing & zine-making culture.
